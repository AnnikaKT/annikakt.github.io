### Student seminar

In the summer term of 2026, Christian Blohmann and I will hold a seminar for Master's students of Bonn university.

The seminar will take place each Wednesday at 14 h c.t. in room 1.008.

The current version of the seminar plan is [as follows](\seminar_plan.pdf). The seminar plan also contains information on the schedule, our expectations for participants and the registration procedure.

#### The preliminary schedule of the seminar is as follows:

* 15.4.2026:
    * Felix Le: Lie groups and Lie algebras
    * Paul Robaschik: Lie group actions on manifolds
* 22.4.2026:
    * Quotients of Lie group actions
    * Equivariant cohomology
* 29.4.2026:
    * So Murata: Properties of equivariant cohomology
    * The Weil algebra
* 13.05.2026:
    * Models for equivariant cohomology
    * The equivariant localisation formula
* 3.6.2026:
    * Lie groupoids and Lie algebroids
* 10.6.2026:
    * Tobias Neuhoff: Lie groupoid actions and principal bundles
    * Morita bibundles
* 17.6.2026:
    * Simplicial manifolds and the Bott-Shulman-Stasheff complex
    * The nerve construction
* 24.6.2026:
    * Pre-stacks over Mfld
* 1.7.2026:
    * Differentiable stacks
    * Differentiable stacks vs. Lie groupoids
* 8.7.2026:
    * Vishal Kannan: Sheaves on differentiable stacks
    * Hypercohomology of differentiable stacks
* 15.7.2026:
    * Cohomology of differentiable stacks via atlases
